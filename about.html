In 2013, massive alien monsters called Kaiju emerge from an interdimensional portal (the Breach) at the bottom of the Pacific Ocean and begin to attack various coastal cities. In 2020, brothers Yancy and Raleigh Becket pilot American Jaeger Gipsy Danger to defend Anchorage from a Category-3 Kaiju codenamed Knifehead.
